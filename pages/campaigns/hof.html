Become the Next Hero of Faerûn

A land of ancient roads, broken empires, and whispered legends.


From the storm-lashed coasts of the Sword Coast to the quiet forests and frontier towns of the North, Faerûn is a realm shaped by ambition, forgotten magic, and the slow grind of history. Every ruined keep has a story. Every trade road hides dangers older than the kingdoms that paved them.


Heroes of Faerûn is a campaign based on the Phandelver & Below: Shattered Obilisk Module from Dungeons & Dragons. This campaign doesn't run exactly how the book is written and offers the party more chances to explore the world, seek out side quests and interacts with the kindoms & factions more than the base module planned. Chef does this to help make his version of phandelver feel different from what everyone else is doing. We've had players who have done the original Lost Mines campaign or have tried the phandelver campaign before and really enjoy the changes i've made to make it feel more original.

About the Campaign

This campaign is ideal for new players, though all skill levels are welcome. Please note that it is a beginner-friendly game with clearly defined expectations.

Rules will be run strictly as written (RAW). Homebrew and third-party content will not be allowed for character creation, and all players must be willing to show patience and understanding toward newer adventurers learning Dungeons & Dragons.

What to Expect:
  • Beginner friendly (New Players Weclome)
  • Exploration, RP, Combat, puzzles, and more.
  • (RAW) Rules as Written 2024 Ruleset
  • full-access to my beyond book collection
  • Tools: Discord, D&D Beyond, Roll20, and bettr20 extension
